Frontend Integration Notes

  

    
1. Use proxy paths: /analysis/api/<endpoint>

    
2. For pivot-ready downloads use format=csv or format=xlsx.

    
3. For plots use format=png (image), format=json (custom charts), or format=script (reproducible python).
